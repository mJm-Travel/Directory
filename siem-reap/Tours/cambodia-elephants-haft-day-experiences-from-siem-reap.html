Tour Description & Additional Info:

Pick up at your hotel in Siem Reap and transfer to Cambodia Elephants sites to start tour.

Here’s a breakdown of the activities at Cambodia Elephant sites: 1. Introduction (25–30 minutes): Start with a knowledgeable guide sharing insights into the sanctuary’s mission, the elephants' lives, and the sanctuary’s efforts to protect them. 2. Meal Preparation for Elephants (20 minutes): Participants prepare nutritious food for the elephants, gaining hands-on experience in understanding their diet. 3. Digestive Health Special Food Preparation (15 minutes): Prepare a special blend aimed at enhancing the elephants’ digestive health. 4. Mud Bath with Elephants (15 minutes): Join the elephants in a mud bath, enjoying playful interaction and learning about their natural behaviors. 5. Bathing Session (20–30 minutes): Refresh alongside the elephants in a bathing session, strengthening the bond with these gentle giants. 6. Relaxation with Seasonal Fruits (30 minutes): Conclude with a relaxed gathering, enjoying fresh fruits and water in the sanctuary’s natural setting.

After enjoyed with elephants for your new experiences and transfer back to your hotel
