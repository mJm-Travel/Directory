Overview

Visit the family home of a local Cretan for an evening of cooking, eating, and cultural exchange. Begin your visit in the vegetable gardens, where you harvest ingredients to use in your recipes. Learn traditional dishes from your hosts, hear the stories behind each, and taste local cheeses. Enjoy views of the Aegean Sea and the mountains, then sit down to a meal you helped prepare, paired with local wine.
  • Enjoy a cooking class and meal in a family home with sea and mountain views
  • Take home digital photos and recipes to commemorate your visit
  • Understand the foundations of Mediterranean cuisine and culture
  • Round-trip hotel transfers from Chania and along the coast to Maleme
