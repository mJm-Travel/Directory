Overview

As amazing as the archaeological sites in Athens are, they’re far from the only Ancient Greek wonders nearby. Join this historical tour and leave the city for a trip to Delphi, once thought to be the center of the world. See the Temple of Apollo—nicknamed the "Navel of the World"—then the Delphi Archaeological Museum. Please note: admission tickets are not included unless you've selected the option when booking.
  • Enjoy round-trip transportation in a luxury, air-conditioned vehicle
  • Learn about the Athenian Stoa and Polygonal Wall from your guide
  • See the statue of Antinoos and bronze Charioteer in the archaeological museum
  • Enjoy guided tours with a licensed, English-speaking guide

Meeting and Pickup

Meeting point

  • Delphi guided tour from Athens - Tag Athens
    Chatzichristou 21, Athina 117 42, Greece

    The meeting point is at the corner of 21 Chatzichristou str & Makrigianni str, Athens 11742, 150 meters away from the metro station ''Acropolis''. Look out for the BLUE umbrella at the meeting point.

End point

  • Melina Mercouri Monument
    Leof. Vasilisis Amalias 54, Athina 105 58, Greece

    The drop-off point is at Leof. Vasilisis Amalias 54, Athens, 105 58, approximately 240 meters away from the metro station ''Acropolis'' (3 min walk). In front of the Melina Mercouri Monument. Open in Google Maps: https://maps.app.goo.gl/33vrjV3E7zEmd9SH6

Additional Info

  • Not wheelchair accessible
  • Near public transportation
  • No heart problems or other serious medical conditions
  • Travelers should have a moderate physical fitness level
  • Please note that tour duration and timings may vary depending on traffic.
  • Please wear weather-appropriate clothing and comfortable shoes.
  • Please bring sunscreen and a hat for your convenience.
  • If you have any heart conditions, respiratory issues, or other medical concerns that may be affected by walking or high-altitude locations, please consult with your doctor before booking.
  • Stay Hydrated & Protected – Delphi can be hot during summer, so we recommend bringing water, sunscreen, and a hat for your comfort.
  • Moderate Walking Involved – The archaeological site includes uneven terrain and steps, so a moderate level of mobility and fitness is required. Comfortable walking shoes are recommended.
  • Please be advised that if your booking does not include tickets, you are responsible for purchasing your own for the 12:00–13:00 time slot. Please note that this is a peak period with limited availability, and having a booking without tickets does not guarantee entry. To avoid disappointment, or the inconvenience of finding the museum at full capacity upon your arrival, we strongly recommend purchasing your tickets in advance.
  • Please note that especially when booking reduced tickets (e.g., youth or child), a valid photo ID or passport must be presented upon request. Without valid ID, you may be required to pay the full adult price on site.
