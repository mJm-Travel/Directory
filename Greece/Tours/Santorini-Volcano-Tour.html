Overview

Santorini Volcano sits on its own islet some way off the coast of Santorini. There are no water taxis or ferries, so a tour is the only way to visit, and tickets often sell out far in advance. Don’t miss your chance to sail into the caldera, swim in the undersea hot springs, walk along the volcano ridge, and discover the volcano’s history and geology: Entry is at your expense.
  • Perfect for time-pressed visitors: See the caldera and volcano in half a day
  • Take the chance to experience undersea mineral springs at Palea Kameni
  • Avoid FOMO: Don’t miss out on witnessing volcanic action up close
  • Guide leads the way up the volcano and explains its history and geology
