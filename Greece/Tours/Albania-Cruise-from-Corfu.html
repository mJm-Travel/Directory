Albania Cruise from Corfu

Corfu, Greece
High interest
Booked 5+ times today.
  • 10 hours (approx.)
  • Pickup offered
  • Mobile ticket
  • Offered in: English

Overview

Make the most of a spare day in Corfu by visiting Albania, beckoning from across the Straits of Corfu. Ride a ferry to Agios Saranda, and enjoy free time to stroll, relax, and get to know this beach-fringed town. Alternatively, opt for a side trip to Butrint to see its UNESCO-listed ancient Greco-Roman ruins, and take in the nearby Ali Pasha Castle. Your tour includes return ferry tickets and hotel pickup and drop-off.
  • Full-day trip to Albania from Corfu Town
  • Soak up views of Albania on a ferry to beachside Agios Saranda
  • Enjoy free time in Saranda to stroll, sightsee, and enjoy the beaches
  • Opt to visit UNESCO-listed ancient Butrint on a side trip (additional cost)
  • Explore Butrint's classical ruins, see the Ottoman Ali Pasha Castle, and enjoy an optional buffet lunch (own expense)
  • Round-trip ferry rides from Corfu, plus hotel pickup and drop-off included
  • Departure tax at own expense

Featured review
Stephen_B
I had a great time visiting Albania and Butrint National Park. The ruins are very interesting and it is set back in a Forrest. The guides are brilliant and direct you and give lots of information. The walk can be difficult for some and the paths are very stony, so could easily trip if not paying attention. The boat doesn’t take long at all, and plenty of seats with a shop to buy beers, drinks and snacks. There is a one off 20 Euro tax when you get onto the boat this is paid to cover the journey there and back. Shout out to the lady on the boat, she was very helpful and informative. You also get lunch in a nice Forrest setting, drinks are extra and they prefer payment in Euros as do most of the places in Albania. On the return to the port side, you get around and hour to explore before heading back. When you arrive back at the Corfu port and go through customs, the return coaches all have the hotels listed on their windscreens, so look out for that. Also, I had an issue in that on my ticket it said 8:45 however my pick up was 6:10am, so the operator very kindly changed the day to the day afterwards at no extra cost, 10/10 for customer service. But please make sure you check the times on the Viator website messages section or junk email in box. Overall great experience, and I would recommend.

The trip takes long enough that you’d have plenty of time to do so.
