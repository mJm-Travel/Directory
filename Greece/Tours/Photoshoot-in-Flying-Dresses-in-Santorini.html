Photoshoot in Flying Dresses in Santorini

Badge of Excellence
Santorini, Greece
  • 1 hour (approx.)
  • Mobile ticket
  • Offered in: English

Overview

A photo shoot with a flying dress billowing over the Santorini rooftops makes the perfect vacation souvenir. Feel the romance on this couples’ photo shoot in beautiful Imerovigli, which includes one flying dress per couple and a total of 40 processed photos. Your photographer will provide both dress and changing facilities. Please note: package is available for solo travelers, but dress hire is chargeable and only 20 photos are included.
  • Immortalize your romance in a stunning Santorini couples flying dress shoot
  • Great choice for honeymoon or engagement photo shoots
  • Photographer shoots up to 250 photos and will share around 40 processed
  • No changing stress—photographer provides portable changing room

What's Included

  • 1 Hour Photoshooting
  • 200-250 photo format jpg
  • photo processed in the lightroom (color correction, contrast, sharpness)
  • 40 photos processed in the lightroom for couple
  • 20 photos processed in the lightroom for single photoshooting
  • couple photo shoot includes one flying dress
  • if 3 ladies or more book, then we provide each girl with a free dress for rent and free assistant
  • one person photoshooting doesn't include rent of flying dress
  • assistant for couple and single photoshoot
  • Pick up and drop off

End point

This activity ends back at the meeting point.
