FAQs

Where can I buy tickets for the tour?
You can purchase tickets either on this website, at most Paris hotels or on the bus at any hop-on hop-off point.
Is the tour commentary available in my language?
Our bus tours feature pre-recorded commentary in the following languages:
  • English
  • French
  • Italian
  • Spanish
  • Russian
  • German
  • Brazilian Portuguese
  • Chinese Mandarin
  • Korean

Our commentary is broadcast via complimentary wired earphones. Simply pick up a pair when you board the bus, plug them in to the seatback in front of you, and tune in to your preferred language channel! Remember to take your earphones with you when you hop off the bus.
If you like, you're welcome to bring your own wired earphones on board.
Where can I start the tour?
You can hop on and hop off our daytime bus tour from any stop. Please visit our Find a Stop page to see stop locations.
What time do tours start and finish?
Depending on the time of the year and on the boarding point, tours operate between 9:45am and 9:15pm. To complete the full route back to your start point, you should board the bus before 5:30pm, as after this time, tours terminate at the Louvre-Pyramide / Big Bus Information Centre.
Please visit our Service Information page for up-to-date information on tour start times and frequency.
How long does a full tour take?
Classic Route - Red: 2 hours, 15 minutes
Which days do the tours operate?
Our Paris bus tours operate daily!
