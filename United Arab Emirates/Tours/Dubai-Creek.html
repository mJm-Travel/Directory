Dubai Dhow Cruise Dinner – Creek



Rated 2.5 Stars, based on 4 Reviews.
A Dhow Cruise Dinner on the Creek is a popular tourist activity in Dubai. A dhow is a traditional wooden boat that has been used for centuries in the Arabian Gulf region for fishing and transportation. Today, these boats have been converted into floating restaurants that offer a unique dining experience. The Creek is a natural saltwater inlet that runs through the heart of Dubai, dividing the city into two parts: Deira and Bur Dubai. The Dhow Cruise Dinner on the Creek takes passengers on a leisurely tour of the waterway while they enjoy a buffet dinner and traditional Arabic entertainment. During the cruise, guests can enjoy stunning views of the city's skyline, including iconic landmarks such as the Burj Khalifa and the Dubai Frame. The dinner buffet typically includes a variety of international and local dishes, including grilled meats, seafood, salads, and desserts. Non-alcoholic beverages are also included in the package.
