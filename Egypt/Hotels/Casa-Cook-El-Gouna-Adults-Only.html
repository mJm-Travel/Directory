About the neighborhood

About this property

Casa Cook El Gouna - Adults Only

Luxury eco-certified hotel near Red Sea
You can look forward to a terrace, a hair salon, and dry cleaning/laundry services at Casa Cook El Gouna - Adults Only. Adventurous travelers may like the cycling at this hotel. Treat yourself to a massage at the onsite spa. Be sure to enjoy a meal at the two on-site restaurants. Stay connected with free in-room WiFi, and guests can find other amenities such as an outdoor entertainment area and 2 bars.
Other perks include:
  • An outdoor pool
  • Free self parking
  • Bike rentals, airport pick-up service (surcharge), and multilingual staff
  • Massage treatment rooms, a gift shop, and outdoor furniture
Room features
All 100 rooms offer comforts such as air conditioning and bathrobes, in addition to thoughtful touches like free WiFi and safes.
More conveniences in all rooms include:
  • LED light bulbs and eco-friendly cleaning products
  • Eco-friendly toiletries, showers, and hair dryers
  • LCD TVs with satellite channels
  • Electric kettles, daily housekeeping, and phones

Awards and affiliations

Eco-Certified Property
This property participates in Green Key (Foundation for Environmental Education), a program that measures the property's impact on one or more of the following: environment, community, cultural-heritage, the local economy.
