Overview

Discover the most popular sights in Washington, DC on this hop-on hop-off bus tour. Drive past many of the capital's top attractions, including the White House, the Washington Monument, the National Mall, and Arlington National Cemetery. Select a 24, 48, or 72 hours pass then hop on at any of the stops on the Red Route and listen to the audio commentary.
  • Explore the city at your own pace on a hop-on hop-off bus tour
  • Discover the best of cherry blossom season during the limited Pink Route
  • Choose a 24 hours pass, a 48 hours pass or opt for a 72 hours day pass
  • The only tour authorized by the national park service and the preferred tour of the cherry blossom festival

Itinerary

The tour begins when you hop on at any of the available stops. Board one of the open-top buses and enjoy the view with recorded audio commentary detailing interesting facts and stories. Learn about the history and happenings of the nation’s capital as the bus rolls along from one location to the next.

Hop-off at any of the attractions for a closer look, then, hop back on when it’s convenient. See the sites on the Red Route (Official National Mall Tour), authorized by the National Park Service and National Mall and Memorial Parks.

Take in all the places that are synonymous with the nation’s capital: The White House, U.S. Capitol, Air and Space Museum, Washington Monument, Jefferson Memorial, FDR Memorial and the Lincoln Memorial are just some of the iconic landmarks. During the spring, you'll also admire the cherry blossoms.

Pick between 1-, 2-, or 3-day passes to fit your schedule. 2-day and 3-day passes include a Monuments Water Taxi Cruise
